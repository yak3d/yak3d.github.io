## Blender & Substance
A very new interest of mine and one of the few art forms I find my brain can comprehend! I'd consider myself still a beginner though most of the assets for Terminus I made myself in Blender and Substance.

I picked up a course by [3D Tutor](https://3dtudor.gumroad.com/) called **Blender 3 to Unreal Engine 5 Dungeon Modular Kitbash**. As of now it looks like the course was delisted but I strongly suggest their courses!

## Unreal Engine
In addition to learning Blender, Unreal Engine 5 has been a recent pickup. The **3D Tutor** courses do touch on Unreal Engine but I wanted a more game focused look.

For this, I picked up **Unreal Engine 5 C++ The Ultimate Game Developer Course** by [Stephen Ulibarri (Druid Mechanics)](https://www.youtube.com/DruidMechanicsGameDevelopment). I'll post my experience with it when I complete it.
